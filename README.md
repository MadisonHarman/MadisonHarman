### 👩‍💻 About Me

- 🎓 First-generation, **fourth-year** student at **Louisiana State University**, **Ogden Honors College**—majoring in **Computer Science** (Software Engineering concentration), minoring in **Digital Media Arts & Engineering in Technology** and **Honors Research**.
- 🌀 Student researcher at LSU's **Stephenson Disaster Management Institute (SDMI)**, working on **POWERCAST**—a hurricane power outage prediction project combining outage data, HURDAT2 storm tracks, Stage IV rainfall, vegetation, and soil moisture pipelines.
- 🛰️ Research assistant under **Dr. Ghawaly**, working on **semantic segmentation**—labeling LiDAR & photogrammetry point clouds for real-world scene reconstruction.
- 🚀 **Break Through Tech AI** ambassador.
- 🤖 Into **AI/ML**—competed in a [Kaggle competition](https://github.com/AI-Studio-Cerebrum/Spring-AI-Studio) on predictive modeling for ADHD diagnosis.
- ☁️ Built an agentic Teams chatbot (**Python** + **Azure Bot Framework**) during a Software Engineering internship at **Chevron**.
- 🚦 Former Research Assistant and Engineering intern at the **Louisiana Transportation Research Center**/**DOTD**, analyzing traffic crash data with **R** and doing research on electric vehicles.
- 📚 Off-screen: video games, classical literature, and psychological horror films.
